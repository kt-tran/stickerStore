Please note:

Do not place items decorated with stickers in a dishwater, oven, or microwave.
Do not place stickers at the bottom or inside a bathtub.
Stickers may not perform well on brick, cinder block or any other porous surfaces.
Prevent placing stickers on rugs, fabrics, clothing, or any other textile as textile fibres may damage the adhesive and make it ineffective.
Since very high and low temperatures can compromise the adhesive and cause stickers to peel, rip, fall off, or damage the surfaces, it is highly advisable to not place stickers in areas where they will be exposed to extreme heat or cold.
